Web Desk

Welcome! This is a website to help people have less clutter on their pages and have a handy website page where they can organize external website links.

Purpose

As a student that has a lot going on at once, I wanted a simple way to navigate across different websites without the constant multi-tab, multi-window internet explorers to go through daily.

Icons provide links to other websites, allowing a simple close and click on a different icon for continuous work as you shift around during your day.

Author

My name is Bradley Larkin and this was my school project. I pushed all my skills and worked on something that I would have and will use.
